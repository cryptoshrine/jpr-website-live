Funding Options

Home Repair Assistance Grant

Up to £5,000

Government grants available for essential roof repairs or replacements for eligible homeowners.

  • Available for those on means-tested benefits
  • For those with disabilities or aged 60+
  • Covers essential roof repairs
  • Limited to £5,000 over three years

Energy Company Obligation

Variable Funding

The ECO scheme requires larger energy suppliers to fund home energy efficiency improvements.

  • Includes roof insulation funding
  • For low-income households
  • Energy-inefficient homes prioritized
  • Apply through energy provider

Great British Insulation Scheme

Full or Partial Funding

Funding for insulation improvements, including roof and loft insulation, to increase energy efficiency.

  • Running until March 2026
  • Available in England, Scotland, and Wales
  • Specific energy efficiency criteria
  • Apply through accredited installers
